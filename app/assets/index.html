Our Story
They met on the staircase. She said "hello" and he made a lame joke. They went their separate ways, never to meet again until the very last two weeks of their freshmen year.
Unfortunately, some stereotypes just aren't true and the girl was frantically searching for someone to help her prep for her math final. Only one smooth, charming genius stepped forward to seize the opportunity. "Uh, yeah, I can math good!"

Who could resist that charm? From then on, they were inseparable.
Before they knew it, 4 years had passed.

On August 15th, 2015, Cecilia was suddenly blindfolded and whisked away during a girls’ night out. When the blindfold came off, she was sitting in front of a laptop with the words 'Press to Start', which launched the retelling of all Eric & Cecilia's adventures through 8-bit form. After playing through the game Eric created for her, Cecilia stepped out onto a rose and candle filled balcony to see the only person in the world who could truly make her happy.

The perfect timing brought two weird people together. What if Cecilia had paid attention in class? What if Eric wasn't goofing off in the lounge? Fortunately, the world may never know!
