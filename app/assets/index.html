Our Story
Unfortunately, some stereotypes just aren't true and Cecilia was frantically searching for someone to help her prep for her pre-calc final during the last week of freshmen year. Only one nerd could stand up to the task and he boldly proclaimed, "Uh, yeah, I can math good!"

Who could resist that charm? From then on, they were inseparable.

Before they knew it, 4 years had passed. On August 15th, 2015, Cecilia was suddenly blindfolded and whisked away during a supposed girls' night out. When the blindfold came off, she was sitting in front of a laptop with the words 'Press to Start', which launched the retelling of all Eric and Cecilia's adventures through 8-bit form. After playing through the game Eric created for her, Cecilia stepped out onto a rose and candle filled balcony to see the only person in the world who could truly make her happy.

The perfect timing brought two weird people together. What if Cecilia had paid attention in class? What if Eric wasn't goofing off in the lounge? Fortunately, the world may never know!
